Threewave is The Multiplayer Game Development Studio.

Our goal is to work closely with the world's finest game studios to provide services that bring the games to the next level. Since 1996, we've delivered award winning content for some of the most popular titles in the first person shooter world.

Threewave's studio is in the heart of Port Coquitlam, British Columbia. We are dedicated to providing secure, reliable and efficient development services. All staff members have access to top notch dual display workstations and enterprise class server technology with redundant offsite encrypted backup technology.
