January 25, 2002
Fragapalooza 2002 Updates - 5:28 am MST - StraT
We have already begun the planning for FP2K2 and we should have the new updated page up by next week or so. The tentative date for this years event is July 18 - July 21 so please mark it on your calenders and plan for it. If all goes well the location will be the same as last year, The Mayfield Trade Center. Registration will begin in late March and those of you who were registered last year will get an e-mail notification just before we put it up.

I want to thank all of you who attend every year and support us. We strive every year to improve each event from the last. If there are any comments, suggestions, or ideas that you may have for Fragapalooza please let either me gil@fragapalooza.com or Gaza gaza@fragapalooza.com know. We would really appreciate any input you guys can give.

Lastly, we will be looking for a few staff members to help with organizing, planning and sponsors. You MUST be at least 21 years of age, be able to attend meetings (in Edmonton, Alberta) and have some attributes you think would benefit Fragapalooza. Contact gil@fragapalooza.com or gaza@fragapalooza.com with a little bit about yourself and how you think you can help us once again make Fragapalooza 2002 a Kick Ass Event!

CHEERZ!
